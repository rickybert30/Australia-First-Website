Australian Candidate Transparency Database

Publicly sourced, factual information on Australia's federal and state politicians and election candidates. Every claim links to its source. This is an information resource, not a scorecard.

A verified position is the member's own stated view or vote. Where a member has no personal position on record, their party's platform is shown, labelled party platform.

Records public statements and conduct only. No ethnicity, ancestry, or heritage is recorded. Citizenship reflects Section 44 constitutional eligibility only. Found an error? Open an issue with a better source.

Member portraits are sourced from Wikimedia Commons (via each member's Wikipedia article) or, where unavailable, from OpenAustralia.org. Click a portrait for its source and image licensing.
